Privacy Policy for Off The Screen

Off The Screen values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Off The Screen does not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or device data.

How We Use Information

Since no data is collected, Off The Screen does not process or use any personal information for any purpose.

Off The Screen does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external service integrations that collect data.

Data Security

Because Off The Screen does not collect or transmit data, there are no risks of data breaches from this app related to user data collection.

Children’s Privacy

Off The Screen is safe for users of all ages. It does not collect or store any information from children.

Changes to This Policy
